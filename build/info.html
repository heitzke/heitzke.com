Hey friend

Despite recent popularity in random terms, I'm not a ninja, rockstar or a badger of any flavor. I'm Mike Heitzke, Birmingham based UI designer, husband, dog father, constant breaker of vehicles, craft beer lover & fitness enthusiast. All kinds of adjectives describe me fairly well, but what I do is craft beautiful & usable interfaces.

I love working within this industry and continuing to develop and grow a day at a time. I've been working at RoR focused shops for the last few years and have really fallen in love with the development style & cycle. HAML & SCSS rock my world, and I'd *actually* like to hear about your open-source project.

You can find me at any of those little links in the footer. If you're lazy, you can check out some work on Dribbble and Forrst. You can also talk to me on Twitter and Facebook
